About Robots

Panchita and Alexcita the robots deliver food and drinks by quietly shuttling down the aisles of the restaurant while customers sit nearby, eating. You might wonder how they work well they have one or two sweeping brushes under the motor and two rolling brushes. When a robot arrives at its destination, customers type a code into their phones to open the lid and retrieve their food. The robots have drawbacks that limit their usefulness for now. They have a small raduis so they dont move very far. The RPA architect will identify opportunities in existing customer business processes to architect and design RPA frameworks/technology. The architect will function as an integrator between the business and technology to create solutions for customers based on business requirements.

Evolution of robots

Evolutionary robotics is an embodied approach to Artificial Intelligence (AI) in which robots are automatically designed using Darwinian principles of natural selection. The design of a robot, or a subsystem of a robot such as a neural controller, is optimized against a behavioral goal (e.g. run as fast as possible)..
